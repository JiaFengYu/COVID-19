How it spreads

People in close contact(about 6 feet). Inhaling respiratory droplets from an infected person coughing or sneezing.

Symptoms

Fever, cough, shortness of breath.

Prevention and Treatment

What To Do If You're Sick

If you catch yourself coughing severely, or if you recently went to one of the "hot" locations, make sure to see your family doctor or any health practitioner you trust. You may not only catch and treat the virus at its early stages, but also prevent those around you from contacting it. If you are cleared by your doctor, phew! Remember to avoid crowded areas, wash your hands regularly, and don't touch your face.

Eliminating Stigmas

This is a stressful time for everyone. For those with family in Wuhan, it can be very scary. Being xenophobic is NOT the solution. Do not bully, do not discriminate. Treat people with respect. This is not the time (and it never is) to belittle others for their race.
